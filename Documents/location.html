Our Location

Grandad's is located in the very town where he raised his family and made some of these recipes for others in the area.

Located right next to the corner of State Street and Sunset Drive in Poplar Bluff, our small, family-owned restaraunt is right in the heart of small town America. After you stop by here, feel free to roam around and get to know our hometown!
